Who we are

The Mess is a four piece songwriting and music production team based in Stockholm, Sweden.

We met at Musikmakarna, Songwriters Academy in Örnsköldsvik and found that the differences in our musical backgrounds were an advantage when it came to music production and songwriting.

We started out in the early winter of 2013.

Achievements

  • Release with Emerentia and the song Wasting water.
  • Songs to be released with Salvatore Ganacci and the Taiwanese artist Genie Chou.
  • Songs on hold with Taped Rai, Anders Bagge, Maryjet, The Kennel and the Japanese boyband Kat-tun.

What's next

We feel like this is the beginning of something great and that the next step should be Los Angeles. Our minds are set and our goals are solid!
